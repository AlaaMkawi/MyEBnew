About Me

Your trusted resource for assisting new parents in caring for their children up to the age of one.

At MyEB, we understand the challenges that come with parenting, especially during the first year. Our mission is to provide support, guidance, and resources to help parents navigate the early stages of their child's development with confidence. Whether you have questions about infant care, feeding, sleep patterns, or developmental milestones, we're here to help. Our team of experts is dedicated to ensuring that parents have the knowledge and support they need to give their children the best start in life.
