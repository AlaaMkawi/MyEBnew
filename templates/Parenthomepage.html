About Us

At MyEB, we are dedicated to providing parents with the tools and resources they need to ensure the health and well-being of their infants up to the age of one. Our team of professionals, including pediatricians and psychologists, is committed to offering comprehensive care and support to families.

With our user-friendly platform, parents can access valuable information, schedule appointments, and connect with healthcare providers seamlessly.
